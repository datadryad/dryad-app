First Steps

Dryad uses ORCID as its primary login method. Please log in using your ORCID account. If you do not have an account, you'll have an opportunity to create one.

If you are a former Dryad or Dash user you will have the opportunity to migrate your data if it does not appear automatically after logging in.

Why ORCID?

ORCID provides a persistent identifier, an ORCID ID, that distinguishes you from other researchers. It provides a mechanism for linking your research outputs and activities to your ID. Learn more at orcid.org.
